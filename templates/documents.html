Documents

This section provides access to the core chapters and supplementary materials of the capstone project. Each document is organized into individual cards, offering a clear overview of the content, including an introduction, review of related literature, methodology, results and discussion, conclusion, recommendations, and appendices. Users can easily view the purpose of each chapter and download the corresponding files for detailed reference.

Chapter 01

Introduction

Introduction serves as the foundation of the capstone project, providing an overview of the study and setting the context for the entire work. It establishes the relevance and importance of the project while introducing the problem, objectives, and scope

Chapter 02

Review of Related Literature and Studies

Chapter 2 of the capstone project reviews existing literature and studies related to the topic. It places the current research within the context of prior work, showing how it connects to and supports the project.

Chapter 03

Methodology

Chapter 3 explains how the study was conducted, ensuring the methodology is appropriate for answering the research questions. It justifies the choices made in research design, data collection, and analysis, ensuring the study's validity and reliability.

Chapter 04

Results and Discussion

Chapter 4 presents and interprets the research findings, linking them back to the research questions and objectives. It helps readers understand the significance of the results and how they contribute to the broader field of study.

Chapter 05

Conclusion and Recommendations

Chapter 5 wraps up the study by summarizing the results and providing conclusions. It offers actionable recommendations based on the research findings and suggests directions for future work.

Appendices

The appendices provide a comprehensive set of supplementary materials that support and clarify the study's methods, data, and findings. They ensure transparency and allow readers to access additional information if needed, without cluttering the main text of the capstone project.
